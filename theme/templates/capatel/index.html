ABOUT US

What's Our Resources Story

Tentron is a company with deep experience in the field of wind turbines, committed to providing sustainable, efficient, and cost-effective power solutions for homes, businesses, and communities worldwide.

Our mission is to propel the advancement of wind energy through our innovative technologies, working towards a greener, more sustainable future.

Our team is made up of a passionate group of renewable energy experts, each a specialist in their field, working tirelessly from engineering to customer service to realize our mission.

Join us in our journey towards a cleaner, greener future.

OUR SERVICES

What We Offer

Wind Energy Consultation

Tentron's team of experts provides detailed wind energy consultations, guiding you through the process of understanding your energy needs, assessing your site's wind resources, and selecting the right wind power system for your specific requirements.

System Design and Installation

We offer comprehensive system design and installation services. Our team will design a wind power system tailored to your site and energy needs, and our experienced technicians will handle the installation, ensuring your system is set up for optimal performance.

Maintenance and Support

Tentron provides ongoing maintenance and support for all our wind power systems. This includes routine inspections, servicing, and system monitoring to ensure your wind power system continues to operate efficiently.

Custom Wind Power Solutions

For commercial and industrial clients with unique energy needs, we offer custom wind power solutions. We design and implement wind power systems specifically tailored to meet the energy demands of your business or industrial process.

Wind Energy Training

We offer training programs for businesses, institutions, and individuals who want to understand more about wind energy. This includes technical training on wind turbine operation and maintenance, as well as broader courses on the benefits and implementation of wind power.

Sustainability Planning and Implementation

Tentron helps businesses and public institutions plan and implement sustainable energy strategies. This includes integrating wind power into existing energy systems, optimizing energy usage, and helping to meet sustainability targets.
